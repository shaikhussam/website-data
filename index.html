Who Is Behind AIONE

A team of healthcare experts, equipped with AI.

AIONE is built by a multidisciplinary team of experienced hospital pharmacy professionals — clinical pharmacists, operations leads and informatics specialists — who have spent years on the front line of hospital practice.

Working hand-in-hand with modern AI tools, the team translates that frontline experience into practical, offline-first applications that ease the complex workflows of a hospital pharmacy — from inventory and formulary to medication safety and clinical decision support.

"Technology should return the pharmacist to the patient, not to the paperwork."
  • Decades of combined hospital pharmacy experience across clinical and operational roles
  • Practising clinicians who understand real ward and dispensary workflows
  • AI-assisted development, from concept to working offline application
  • Grounded in recognised guidelines — ACC/AHA, IDSA, KDIGO, USP and more
  • Coverage spanning clinical, safety, operations, quality and research
  • Every tool built offline-first: no server, no installation, no cloud dependency
  • A shared mission to simplify complex hospital pharmacy workflows
AI Skills

A working library of AI-built clinical software.

Our team has developed an extensive collection of AI-driven, single-file HTML clinical tools and applications. Highlights include the Online Formulary; a Pharmacy Downtime System with dual formulary support and custom QR encoding; drug interaction checkers; an anticoagulation management tool; antibiogram decision-support; USP 797 compliance utilities; budgeting and analytics dashboards; a video-conferencing app (Portal); and a suite of 3D clinical training games (Cardiac Rescue, Code Blue, PharmaCity, ECG Academy, and more). The team has also created stewardship and drug-selection guidance tools under the Formulary Price Intelligence initiative.
